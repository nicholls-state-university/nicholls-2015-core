Welcome

Welcome to the Office of University Marketing and Communications. Our mission is to promote Nicholls State University through marketing/advertising, media relations, publications, photography, videography and web development. Through all of these areas, the Marketing and Communications team works to communicate high-priority messages with the goal of enhancing the visibility and image of the university to key internal and external audiences.


Nicholls Branding Guide

Visit nicholls.edu/branding to download Nicholls logos and learn about proper university logo usage, official colors, preferred typefaces and more.
